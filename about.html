    Tunnel Vision Weekly: In the beginning...

    What is Tunnel Vision Weekly?

    Tunnel Vision Weekly is a biweekly (formerly weekly) newspaper, distributed every other Monday in paper and published digitally at the end of the same week. We started publication in March 2025, and currently have 6 sections. Our current circulation comfortably sits at around 40.

    Our sections!

    What's new in the box(es)
    A section all about the tech dumps. Are you curious about what people threw out over the past week? That's where you look. Also included is a little opinion piece about the latest stuff that happened, as well as the find(s) of the week: stuff we find in the tech dumps that are saved and put into the DCC tech dump as a showcase for the week.
    State of the Art
    A section all about the art of the tunnels connecting most of the main campus buildings. All the latest drawings that popped up over the past week, and an expert analysis of what the drawings mean.
    Tunnel Vision: Out of the Tunnel
    A section about anything else, basically. In Issue #2 we featured an article submitted to TVW as the inaugural article for this section. Issue #3 focused on the art of the past, also submitted to TVW.
    Subterranean Homesick Blues
    A section authored by Pi focusing on the underground music scene and the people in it. It's a new section for Fall '25!
    Geologic Fever
    A section authored by S.S. Illite about rocks and geologic features around campus. It's a new section for Fall '25, with its debut in Issue #9!
    Puzzles n' Stuff
    A section about games and stuff that you can play once you're done with your paper (or while you're reading if you're cool enough). It's a new section for Fall '25.

    Who are we?

    We are a group of RPI students dedicated to providing the RPI community with a (bi)weekly news source, or at least an entertainment source. As of Fall '25, we have 3 people confirmed as regular writers!
